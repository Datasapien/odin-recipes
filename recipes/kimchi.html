Kimchi Fried Rice

Description

Hailing from one of the foodiest nations on the planet, kimchi fried rice is a staple of Korean cuisine. It's softly spicy and sour flavours till tantalise your taste buds and leave you wanting more. Sound good? Read on to find out how!

Ingredients

  • rice
  • kimchi
  • sesame oil
  • tuna
  • egg
  • onion
  • spring onion
  • sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Cook your rice on a rice cooker (if you don't have one, go and buy one ... you'll thank me later)
  2. Pour sesame oil into a pan
  3. Fry onions until they begin to soften
  4. Place kimchi, tuna and spring onions in with the onions and gently fry
  5. Place portions of rice into the pan
  6. Add kimchi juice to the pan (and extra sesame oil if needed)
  7. Fry an egg
  8. Plate up the fried rice and place the egg on top
  9. Sprinkle the sesame seeds
  10. Enjoy!
