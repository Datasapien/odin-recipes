Crunchy Crunchy Toasty

Description

If you're anything like me, the last thing you want to do is spend 2+ hours in the kitchen after a long day of crowd control at school. Make a sandwich.

Ingredients

  • bread (Warburton's Seeded Batch)
  • strong cheddar cheese
  • butter
  • cucumber
  • ham
  • lettuce
  • mustard
  • cherry tomatos

Steps

  1. Put your bread in the toaster and wait for it to pop
  2. Wait for the bread to cool down then add generous amounts of butter to one slide and your mustard to the other
  3. Add your ham, then cheese, then cucumber, then salad, then tomatos
  4. Add your mustardy slice on top of the mountain of yum
  5. Slice your sandwich in half (I prefer down the middle but you can do diagonally if you so prefer)
  6. Enjoy!
